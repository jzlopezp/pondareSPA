About

The LSPU-SPCC College of Computer Studies, abbreviated as LSPU-SPCC CCS, is a prominent educational institution situated in Barangay Del Remedio, San Pablo City, Laguna. Known for its commitment to excellence in computer education, the college offers a wide range of programs and courses designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to thrive in today's technology-driven world.


The curriculum at LSPU-SPCC CCS is carefully crafted to align with industry standards and trends, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the demands of the job market. From computer programming and software development to cybersecurity and digital marketing, the college offers a comprehensive education that prepares students for diverse career opportunities in the field of computer studies.

Courses

Information Technology

The Information Technology (IT) course at LSPU-SPCC College of Computer Studies (CCS) is a comprehensive program that equips students with essential knowledge, skills, and practical experience for success in the rapidly evolving tech industry. Starting with core concepts in computer science and IT fundamentals like programming languages, data structures, and computer networks, students progress to advanced topics such as software development, database management, web development, networking, cybersecurity, and IT project management. The curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, industry-relevant skills, and real-world applications, preparing graduates for diverse roles like software developers, systems analysts, network administrators, cybersecurity specialists, and IT project managers. Through internships and industry exposure programs, students gain valuable experience, establish professional connections, and become well-equipped to thrive in the dynamic and competitive IT landscape.

Computer Science

The Computer Science course at LSPU-SPCC College of Computer Studies (CCS) is a comprehensive program that equips students with a strong theoretical understanding and practical skills in various aspects of computer science. Covering fundamental topics such as algorithms, data structures, programming languages like Java, C++, and Python, as well as database management using MySQL, Oracle, and SQL Server, students gain expertise in software development, coding practices, and database administration. The curriculum also includes advanced subjects like software engineering principles, object-oriented programming, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science, providing a well-rounded education in cutting-edge technologies. Through practical projects, coding exercises, and lab sessions, students develop problem-solving abilities and critical thinking skills necessary for roles in software development, systems analysis, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and research in both academic and industrial settings.
